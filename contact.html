How the call works

A real conversation, not a sales pitch.

It's simple, and there's no pressure. We get on a call, and if you've sent a short video of yourself playing, we watch it together. I tell you what I see, live: what's working, what I'd change, and the one or two things that would make the biggest difference to your game.

You walk away with a real feel for how I coach, and honest feedback you can use straight away. And I get a clear sense of whether I can genuinely help you, because now it isn't only about what you feel and what you want, it's that plus what I actually see on the court. That's what makes it powerful.

If it's a fit, I'll show you how we'd work together. If it's not, no problem, you'll still leave with something useful. Prefer to get a feel first? My free ebooks and weekly videos on YouTube are a good place to start, or reach me directly at benoit@ready2perform.com.
